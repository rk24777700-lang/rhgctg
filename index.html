About Us

RHG CTG (Rohingya Chittagong) is a community organization dedicated to supporting, educating, and empowering people through unity, positive values, and community service. We believe that together we can build a stronger, cleaner, and more respectful community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage our community to live with cleanliness, discipline, education, and respect. We aim to help people develop confidence, improve their daily lives, adopt positive habits, and become responsible members of society. Through teamwork, learning, and community service, we strive to create opportunities for a brighter future for everyone.

Our Focus

  • 🤝 Community Support
  • 👨‍🎓 Youth Development
  • 📚 Education & Awareness
  • 🧹 Clean Environment & Healthy Living
  • 💼 Leadership & Life Skills
  • ⚽ Sports & Cultural Activities
  • ❤️ Volunteer & Social Services
  • 🌍 Unity, Respect & Community Development
